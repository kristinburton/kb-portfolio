About Me

For over 12 years, coding was my main focus. I started out building B2C websites, then transitioned into the B2B space at Zendesk, where I worked as a front-end developer. My goal was always the same: bring designs to life with pixel-perfect precision, delivering high-quality results that met both creative and budget goals.

But at the heart of it all, design has always been my true passion.

Front-end development gave me a unique balance—I got to flex my logical, problem-solving side while watching creative ideas take shape on the screen. Over time, I found myself more drawn to the design process itself: crafting the visuals, shaping the experience, and making sure every detail felt just right.

At Zendesk, I began designing custom help centers for clients, which deepened my love for UX and visual storytelling. That eventually led me to a contract role at Go Envoy, where I now focus primarily on web design, with occasional development work.

Websites and apps have become my creative playground. I love designing experiences that feel intuitive, beautiful, and purposeful. Whether I'm laying out a page, choosing a color palette, or pairing the perfect typography, I'm always thinking about how it makes someone feel when they land on a site.

I believe we're always evolving—and I'm constantly learning and growing as a designer. Right now, I'm focused on sharpening my skills, exploring new design trends, and creating work that's both functional and inspiring.
